Gordon Gekko

Gordon Gekko(played by Michael Douglas’s, his Oscar-winning role)
in WallStreet is a Slick-haired, Armani-clad corporate-raider Gekko will stop at nothing
to make more millions: using inside information,
plundering pension funds, bribing, blackmailing and breaking the law.
Free of morals and contemptuous of his fellow man,
he lives up — or rather down — to his reptilian name.
